Deck -> contains 52 cards, list with 52 objects
Cards -> has a array of values from 1->13, Ace is valued 1 AND 11
On game start shuffle order of deck.
Take 2 cards out of the deck, Give one to the player and one to the dealer.
Display Dealer Card.
Ask player to hit, stand or fold.
Have a player turn go by either taking one card from the deck or not.
Then give dealer another card.
Display Dealer Card
Repeat until someone busts, or dealer reaches value limit (16)
